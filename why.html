What We Do

How We Do It
1

CLIENT FIRST

We stop for nothing but to resolve our clients’ problems. Our dedicated team can focus on specific situations, allowing us to quickly understand how our clients’ businesses work and what they need. 


2

EXTREME PROFESSIONALISM

We do not act without a plan. Setting goals, planning and execution are musts for every work we undertake. Eliminating or minimising client expectation gaps through clear communication is another way of maintaining our professionalism. We welcome feedback.


3

INTEGRITY

We maintain transparency and accept responsibility in all our acts. We can be trusted with confidential information. We do what is right and just. If we commit to something, we follow through. 


4

HIRE AND RETAIN TALENT

We pay attention into detail when identifying our clients’ staffing needs and initiate our spot-on recruitment process. Keeping long standing relationship with the acquired talent is a secret behind the satisfaction of our clients. 


5

COMMITMENT TO TEAM

Anything is possible if team members commit to work hard and learn. ER commits to provide them with coaching, motivation, and resources. We lean on each other. We help each other succeed at work, stay healthy, and live fulfilled personal lives. 


6

CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

Our team of specialists possess up to date technical and subject specific knowledge. We critically study our processes and create better, smarter solutions.
